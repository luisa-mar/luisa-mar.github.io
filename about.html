About Me

My name is Luisa Blom. I graduated from Gymnasium in the summer of 2020, where my focus lied on art, which is why I had the chance to go to the Zürcher Hochschule der Künste for a four month precours so I can get in to art school later this year!

Art has always been a passion of mine and is my comfort zone, so I am happy to be able to share my work with you today.

Currently I am working in retail in a bakery shop in my neighbourhood, gathering new experiences and getting to make new friendships.

My dream would be to be able to go to art school and study Art Education to then go to a school and show how magical and mentally healing painting can be.
