My name is Luisa Marie Blom. I'm an artist from Zurich, Switzerland starting my first year of art education in September in Basel. I graduated from Gymnasium with my major in art in the summer of 2020. In the last months of that year i went to a preliminary course at the ZHdK while working in a bakery, gathering new experiences and making new friends. In april I went to Montpellier France after passing all my interviews for four major art schools in Switzerland. In Montpellier I am improving my French and making new stories to tell at home.
Art has always been a passion of mine and has always given me a certain pride and comfort, so I am happy to be able to share my work with you today. ♥
