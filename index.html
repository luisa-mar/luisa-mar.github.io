My name is Luisa Marie Blom. I'm an artist from Zurich, Switzerland in my first year of art education at the Fachhochschule Nordwestschweiz in Basel. At my new school, I am gathering new experiences, making art, pursuing new hobbies, reaching for new goals, and making new friends.
In April 2021, I went to Montpellier France, after passing all my interviews for four major art schools in Switzerland. In Montpellier improved my French language skills and made new stories to tell at home while writing the code for this website!
Art has always been a passion of mine and has always given me a certain pride and comfort, so I am happy to be able to share my work with you today. ♥
